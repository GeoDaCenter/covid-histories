# Resources

The pandemic has been a difficult and traumatic time for many people. This page provides resources to help you if you feel stressed, overwhelmed, or need help while you record your story.

## Crisis Support
- [Crisis Text Line Text](https://www.crisistextline.org/) or text HOME to 741741
- [No Shame on U 24/7 Hotline](http://www.noshameonu.org/chicagoland) or via phone 1-800-273-8255 
- [SAMSHA Hotline](https://www.samhsa.gov/find-help/national-helpline) or via phone 1-800-662-4357
- [Suicide Prevention Lifeline](https://suicidepreventionlifeline.org/) or via phone 1-800-273-8255
